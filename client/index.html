Briefing

How to play

Objective

Hiders wear a camo pattern and vanish into matching terrain. Seekers hunt with paintballs. A hit turns a hider into a seeker. Roles rotate across five rounds.

Controls

WASD Move LMB / Space Fire (seeker) Tab Swap role (practice only)

Hiding

  • Moving makes your shimmer obvious.
  • Standing still for 2s tightens the shimmer.
  • Matching terrain (Jungle → dense green, Desert → riverbank) makes you harder to spot.

Seeking

  • Fully visible; fire paintballs on a short cooldown.
  • During prep, you stay in the spawn pen and cannot see hiders.
  • Hit a hider to infect them — they join the hunt.

Read the field

Jungle camo · zones A & C    Desert camo · zone B    Seeker

Faint zone tints on the map mark Deep Jungle, Riverbank, Forest Edge, and Clearing. Score for surviving as a hider, last hider standing, and clean sweeps as seekers.
